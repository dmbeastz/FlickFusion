/movies: Retrieves all movies.
/movies/<int:movie_id>: Retrieves a specific movie by its ID.
/series: Retrieves all series.
/series/<int:series_id>: Retrieves a specific series by its ID.
/movieinfo: Retrieves all movie information.
/movieinfo/<int:movie_id>: Retrieves movie information by movie ID.
/seriesinfo: Retrieves all series information.
/seriesinfo/<int:series_id>: Retrieves series information by series ID.
/trailers: Retrieves all trailers.
/trailers/<int:trailer_id>: Retrieves a specific trailer by its ID.
